EVCC 'Chargeboard' software

At ISU's solar car team, PRISUM, I specifically worked on the EVCC software for P15 Eliana. The system mainly functioned as a means to allow our car to be race ready and charge via standard J177 EV chargers you could find in places like your local walgreens.

This project was mainly done in C, with a little bit of Assembly.
